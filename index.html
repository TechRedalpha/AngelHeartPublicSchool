Angel Heart Public School

A Detailed Journey of Angel Heart Public School

Dankuni village, located in the Kolkata, spans 203 hectares. On November 20, 2020, the school was established with a humble thatched roof of coconut leaves under the name "Angel Heart Public School." The founding principal, Mr. A. ABC, made significant contributions to its establishment, supported by two teachers, M.K. Udaris and P. Udaris.

In the beginning, the school had only one blackboard, 12 benches, one desk, one logbook, and a daily attendance register. It started with about 83 male students. Later, its structure was modified to accommodate female students. By 1937, the school split into two institutions: one for boys and one for girls. Mr. A. Samaranayake continued as the boys' school principal, while Mrs. Pathiratne Jayasinghe became the girls' school principal. However, in the same year (1937), the two schools were merged into a co-educational institution under the principalship of Mr. D. Sugathadasa. In 1996,Angel Heart Public School was upgraded to a NEXT GEN school.

Formerly known as "Angel Heart Public School" by the community, it has now become a national school. With a history spanning 125 years,Angel Heart Public School has had 30 principals over the years. From 2013 to 2023, the position was held by Mr. A. Ranjith. Currently, the school accommodates around 700 students and a faculty of about 65 teachers. Initially offering only Arts and Commerce streams for Advanced Level, it expanded to include Science and Mathematics streams in 2013. As of 2024, the new principal, Mr. Sujith Palitha Gallage, has taken charge of the school.

The Foundation of Our Students' Success

Alumni Association

The Alumni Association of Angel Heart Public School was established to strengthen the long-lasting relationships within our school community. This association helps maintain essential relationships for the success of both current and future generations by utilizing the experiences and guidance of senior alumni. Over decades, the strong support of our alumni has been a fundamental component of the school's development. Through this, we nurture successful citizens for society.

Arts Society

The Arts Society ofAngel Heart Public School provides an essential platform for promoting students' creativity and skills. Membership in the Arts Society allows students to develop and showcase their talents in the fields of fine arts, performing arts, or other creative areas. We believe that the artistic journey of students will not only lead to their future professional success but also contribute positively to society.

Literary Society

The Literary Society of Angel Heart Public School has helped create a meaningful world through the ideas of our students, showcasing their human projects through heartfelt insights. Members of the Literary Society express all their profound abilities through poetry, storytelling, and analytical writing, and participate in literary discussions and workshops that help expand their skills. We are committed to developing lifelong literary excellence.

Answers to Your Questions

Our school began with a humble heritage, and has come a long way with a rich history and educational excellence. After a modest start, we have achieved significant growth through the vision of skilled teachers and effective management practices. Our educational approach provides access to numerous options and modern resources in subjects such as mathematics, science, commerce, technology, arts, sports, and extracurricular activities, ensuring holistic development of students for success in all fields. Developing leadership and social connections through various associations and organizations is one of our top priorities. With our vision, deep educational insight, and creative thinking, we aim for excellence.

Our school offers various extracurricular activities for students to enhance their talents and skills. These activities include sports teams, music groups, and creative associations, allowing students to join and develop their abilities in areas of interest. Our sports programs provide opportunities for physical and mental well-being through team-based engagement. The art association, with its creative spirit, and the literary association with poetry, storytelling, and writing, help create a world that captivates the hearts of students. All these activities help build a diverse environment for our students, fostering their leadership and skills development.

Parental involvement is vital at our school. Parents have the opportunity to join school boards and event coordination committees, offering direct assistance to decision-making and special events. Additionally, parents are invited to participate in teachers' meetings, special gatherings, and school sports events. They are also encouraged to provide guidance for creative school events and strategies and, as necessary, contribute to the school's guidance efforts. All these avenues help parents become successful supporters of their children's education and the school community.
